Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Tempo di Incanto ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Social media access tokens (to publish content on your behalf)
  • Video and image files you submit for publishing
  • Basic account information from connected platforms (username, page ID)

3. TikTok Data

When you connect your TikTok account, we access only the permissions you explicitly grant. We use TikTok's Content Posting API solely to upload and publish videos on your behalf. We do not:

  • Access your TikTok messages or private data
  • Share your TikTok data with third parties
  • Store TikTok user data beyond what is operationally necessary

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Publish content to your connected social media accounts
  • Authenticate with social media platform APIs
  • Provide status updates on your publications

5. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to external servers controlled by Tempo di Incanto. All processing happens on your own machine.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to interact with the social media platform APIs you have authorized.

7. Data Retention

You can revoke access and delete your data at any time by disconnecting your social media accounts from our service or by deleting the application and its configuration files.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data associated with your use of our service. Contact us at tempodiincanto@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at tempodiincanto@gmail.com.
